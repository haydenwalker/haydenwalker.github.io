Getting to Santa Fe

Flights

New Mexico has two main airports: Albuquerque (ABQ) and Santa Fe (SAF). ABQ has non-stop service from 24 different cities and is easy to fly into and find rental cars, but is farther from the Courtyard Marriott hotel (~1 hour) and venue than it's smaller counterpart SAF. The Santa Fe airport only flies to and from 3 cities (Phoenix, Dallas, and Denver) but is very conveniently located to both the venue and hotel (~15 minutes).

Getting Around

The Hacienda Doña Andrea is about 40 minutes from the Courtyard Marriott hotel in the foothills south of Santa Fe. The route to the Hacienda includes a 1-mile section of well maintained dirt road leading to the guest parking. It is recommended that guests carpool in rented vehicles as taxi/Uber/Lyft drivers in Santa Fe are sparse. At the Santa Fe airport, Hertz and Avis have locations at the terminal, while Enterprise and Budget have off-airport locations nearby. At the Albuquerque Airport, all the above mentioned rental car companies are available at the terminal as well as many other options including National, Dollar, Alamo and Thrifty.
